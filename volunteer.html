Volunteer FAQ

Do I need experience?

No. Training and guidance can be provided before you start.

Can TU Dublin students volunteer?

Yes. Bia Box is a student-led project and welcomes student volunteers.

What do volunteers do?

Volunteers help with sorting, packing, quality control, and preparing boxes.

Can volunteering count towards hours?

Yes, volunteering may help with student volunteer hour requirements depending on your course or programme.
