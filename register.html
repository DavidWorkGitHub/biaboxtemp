How our drops work

Bia Box works on a drop-style model. Registering interest helps us understand demand, plan weekly boxes, and contact households when availability opens.

One-time or monthly

You can choose whether you are interested in a one-time €5 box or the €15 monthly subscription option.

Weekly availability

Boxes depend on the surplus food available each week, so contents and availability can change.
