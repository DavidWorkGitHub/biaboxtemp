Our Mission

Supporting people. Reducing food waste.

Bia Box is a subscription-based surplus food redistribution service that delivers quality surplus food while users only pay for delivery. We believe reducing food waste is a shared responsibility and that surplus food should support people rather than go to waste.

  • Reduce unnecessary food waste.
  • Support households through affordable food access.
  • Lower CO₂ emissions by redistributing edible surplus food.
  • Create a future where good food is never wasted.

How it works

Fresh surplus food, rescued and redistributed.

FoodCloud helps connect surplus food with people and organisations who can put it to good use. Bia Box takes that mission further by packing affordable boxes and delivering them directly to local families.

Environmental Impact

Reducing food waste helps reduce CO₂ emissions.

When edible food is wasted, the resources used to grow, transport, store, and dispose of it are wasted too. By redistributing surplus food, Bia Box helps prevent unnecessary emissions and supports a more circular food system.

🌱 Less unnecessary food production waste
♻️ Less food sent to disposal
🚚 Better use of existing supply chains
🍽️ More edible food used for people
