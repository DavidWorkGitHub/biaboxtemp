Our Weekly Drop

What Bia Box delivers every week.

Bia Box currently delivers 15 boxes per week, and we plan to keep expanding as more families register interest and more surplus food partners come on board.

Every box is different because the mix of items changes weekly depending on the surplus food available. Each weekly drop helps make sure good food is used to support people rather than going to waste.

How Stripe payments work

When you click order or subscribe, you are taken to Stripe Checkout. Stripe securely processes your card payment and manages the subscription checkout process. Bia Box does not store your card details.

Boxes FAQ

How many boxes can I receive?

Each subscription is designed for one Bia Box per week, depending on weekly surplus food availability.

What comes in a Bia Box?

Every box is different. The mix of items changes weekly depending on the surplus food available from our partners.

Who does Bia Box work with?

Bia Box works with FoodCloud partners, Enactus TU Dublin, TU Dublin, Barry's Tea, volunteers, and community supporters.

How do Stripe payments work?

Stripe securely processes one-time and monthly payments. Bia Box does not store your card details.
