FAQ

Most asked questions.

What is Bia Box?

Bia Box is a surplus food redistribution delivery service focused on reducing food waste.

Where does the food come from?

Bia Box collects surplus food from FoodCloud partners.

How does Bia Box reduce CO₂ emissions?

Food waste creates unnecessary emissions from production, transport, storage, and disposal. By rescuing surplus food, Bia Box helps avoid waste and reduce environmental impact.

How do payments work?

Payments are processed securely through Stripe Checkout using the one-time or monthly payment links.

Can I subscribe monthly?

Yes. The monthly food package is €15 per month through Stripe.

How can I contact Bia Box?

Email biabox.ireland@gmail.com or message us on Instagram or LinkedIn.
